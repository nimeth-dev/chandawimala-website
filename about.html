Our History

Founded in 1986 under the patronage of Sri Jayawardhenaramaya, Chandawimala Daham Pasala began with a single classroom and twelve devoted students. Today it stands as one of the most respected Dhamma schools in the Western Province, carrying the flame of the Sasana across generations.

🌅

Our Vision

To cultivate a generation of mindful, compassionate citizens grounded in Buddhist values — young people who carry the light of the Dhamma into every corner of society and uphold the dignity of the Sasana for centuries to come.

🪷

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality Dhamma education through skilled teachers, rich learning resources, and a nurturing environment — honouring the Triple Gem and inspiring students to live by the Five Precepts with joy and understanding.
