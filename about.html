Our Journey & Heritage

Siri Chandawimala Dhamma School, established on 5 April 1974 in Delgahamedilla Village within the Meerigama Divisional Secretariat Division, is a respected institution centered around the Sri Jayawardhanaramaya Temple.

For more than five decades, the school has continued to grow with a steadfast commitment to Buddhist education, nurturing generations of students who are enriched not only with knowledge but also with strong moral values, positive attitudes, and essential life skills. Through this holistic approach, the school has proudly contributed responsible, compassionate, and capable individuals to society.

Recognizing the evolving needs of education, Siri Chandawimala Dhamma School introduced a competency-based, activity-oriented learning approach in 2023. This innovative educational model moves beyond the limitations of traditional examination-focused learning by emphasizing the development of students' character, practical abilities, creativity, and critical thinking alongside academic knowledge.

We believe that meaningful progress is achieved by building upon cherished traditions while embracing thoughtful innovation. Inspired by this vision, we continuously seek new and effective ways to enrich Dhamma education without losing sight of the timeless values upon which our school was founded.

Our ultimate mission is to inspire every student to grow into a person of wisdom, compassion, integrity, and service—a modern-day Siddhartha, whose life brings purpose, kindness, and lasting value to the world.
