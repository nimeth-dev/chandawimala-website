About Our Daham School

Rooted in tradition, growing with purpose.

Our Journey & Heritage

Founded in 1986 under the patronage of Sri Jayawardhenaramaya, Chandawimala Daham Pasala began its noble journey with a single classroom and just twelve devoted students. In those early days, classes were held under the shade of the Bodhi tree, laying a foundation of simplicity and deep devotion to the Dhamma.

Through unwavering dedication by our founding monks and the local community, the school steadily grew. The addition of the main Dhamma Mandiraya in 1995 marked a significant milestone, allowing us to accommodate hundreds of students and establish a highly structured curriculum.

Today, it stands as one of the most respected Dhamma schools in the Western Province. Every Sunday, our temple grounds come alive with students eager to learn, carrying the flame of the Sasana across generations and ensuring the teachings of the Supreme Buddha continue to thrive in the modern world.

Our Vision

To cultivate a generation of mindful, compassionate citizens grounded in Buddhist values — young people who carry the light of the Dhamma into every corner of society and uphold the dignity of the Sasana for centuries to come.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality Dhamma education through skilled teachers, rich learning resources, and a nurturing environment — honouring the Triple Gem and inspiring students to live by the Five Precepts with joy and understanding.
