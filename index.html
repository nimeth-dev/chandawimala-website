Welcome Message

A Message from the
Principal Teacher

❧
"The teachings of the Buddha are not merely words on a page — they are a living path, walked step by step, breath by breath, generation by generation."

With profound reverence and warmth, we welcome you to Chandawimala Daham Pasala, established under the sacred guidance of Sri Jayawardhenaramaya Temple. For decades, our school has nurtured young minds in the timeless wisdom of the Dhamma.

Whether you are a student, a parent, or a devotee, you are part of our sangha. Together, we walk the Noble Eightfold Path toward wisdom, compassion, and peace.

— Ven. Chandawimala Thero, Principal
